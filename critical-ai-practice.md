# Critical AI Practice

Critical AI practice centers on thoughtful engagement with machine learning and automated systems.

- Evaluate model bias, limitations, and transparency
- Consider cultural, social, and environmental impacts
- Design human-centered workflows and safeguards
- Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration

Use this page to articulate values and best practices for working with AI responsibly.
